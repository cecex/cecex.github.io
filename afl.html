AFL Team Canada International Cup


I was fortunate enough to be given the opportunity to design Team Canada's Men's and Women's Jerseys while they competed in the 2014 and 2017 AFL International Cup. The team wanted their jerseys to represent their home while they were in Australia, as well as stand out against opposing teams.
