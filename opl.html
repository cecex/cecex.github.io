My Role

As the Graphic Design Assistant at Oakville Public Library (OPL) for the summer of 2016, I was in charge of creating and implenting graphical content that could be used for promoting TD Summer Reading Club at all Oakville Library Branches.

Project Overview

The illustrations for the 2016 program were done by artist Virginie Egger, which I was able to modify to suit the needs of OPL while keeping their visual identity and adhering to AODA compliancy. I created promotional content including; posters, calendars, lawn signage, social media posts, web adverts, and most notably, window clings for four OPL Branches. I started by designing several mockups for the staff and program coordinators to review and continue working with the ones they liked most. From there, the final window clings were given a call to action, and were seen on OPL windows for the 2016 summer months.
